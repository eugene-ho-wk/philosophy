Eugene Ho &
the nature of mind

Nanyang Technological University · Singapore

I work on the philosophy of mind, language, and the metaphysics of intentionality. My research draws on analytic philosophy and the history of twentieth-century thought to examine how mental and linguistic representations acquire their content.

About

I am a PhD student at the department of philosophy at New York University. I’m also currently affiliated with Nanyang Technological University under the HIPS scheme.

I work in the intersection of philosophy of language, aesthetics, and metaphysics. I'm particularly interested in the philosophy of fiction, and I'm currently thinking about how genre interacts with conditional reasoning in fictive discourse.
